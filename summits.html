BADCamp Summits

Summits allow attendees from specific industries or with specific skills to collaborate freely and dive deep into the issues that matter to them.

Registration is first come, first served and part of the BADCamp registration process, so sign up today!

Tuesday, October 20

Drupal 8 Sprints

Tuesday, October 20 to Sunday, October 25

The first Drupal 8 release Candidate is out! Come port modules and themes to Drupal 8 so the community will be ready when Drupal 8 is released!

Learn more

Thursday, October 22

Backdrop CMS Summit

Thursday, October 22

Backdrop is a new Content Management System based on the Drupal you know and love, but with a new mission that aims to decrease the cost of ownership, to empower more people to do more things on the web.

Learn more

Non-Profit Summit

Thursday, October 22

The BADCamp Nonprofit Summit (NPS) is back in Berkeley for 2015, with even more opportunities for nonprofits and developers to collaborate, learn, and grow! We’ve got a full day of case studies, presentations, and small group breakout sessions, all led by nonprofit tech experts. Come and discover new tools and strategies, learn how to use them, and make contacts with other members of the Drupal Nonprofit community!

Non-Profit Summit Schedule8:00 - 9:00am: Registration
9:00 - 9:15am: Morning Introductions
9:15 - 10:00am: Lightning Talks

Learn more

Friday, October 23

Configuration Management and DevOps Summit

Friday, October 23

Is Configuration Management your favorite feature of Drupal 8? Are you interested in doing continuous integration? Do you want to easily export all of your Drupal configuration to code? Interested in building a best practice continuous integration and deployment solutions? New this year, the Configuration Management and DevOps Summit will be focused on how Drupal 8's Configuration Management system works, how to integrate it with a continous integration system and other Devops magic, and what developers can do to extend its power through contributed modules and custom code.

Learn more

Content Authoring Experience Summit

Friday, October 23

The Content Authoring Summit aims to bring together maintainers of various modules key to an awesome authoring experience in Drupal: layouts, media, previews, workflow, in-place editing, etc. and work through architectural / integration issues side-by-side, with whiteboards and healthy debate, with the goal to make the process of making a rich Drupal 8 authoring experience much, much easier than in the past.

Learn more
