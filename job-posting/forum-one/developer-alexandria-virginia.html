Developer in Alexandria, Virginia

Company: 
Forum One

Are you a developer who loves love working with open source platforms like, Drupal and WordPress? Join Forum One and help us build web solutions for organizations doing important work around the world!  

What you’ll do:

  • Build web sites and applications by writing code (PHP, HTML, CSS, Javascript) and configuring content management and social publishing software (Drupal, Wordpress).
  • Estimate the level of effort of development for individual site features (stories).
  • Attend relevant industry meetups and events and presents relevant topics.
  • Contribute to the Forum One technical blog.

What you bring to the table:

  • Experience building entire features for moderately complex web sites or applications using PHP, HTML, CSS and Javascript.
  • Experience writing basic SQL and create db's with MySQL/SQL.
  • Experience using GIT

Why you’ll love it here:

  • All employees are owners
  • 401(k) match
  • 25 days paid time off to start
  • Flexible work hours
  • Killer benefits & professional development
  • Amazing, world-changing clients you can check out here
  • Snacks, healthy ones and not so healthy ones
  • Personal innovation budget
  • Full staff retreats

About Forum One:

Our team combines policy and passion to address the world’s most pressing problems. For nearly two decades we have focused our work on mission-driven, problem-solving organizations. Since 1996, we’ve been passionately committed to helping our clients extend their influence. We’re headquartered in Alexandria, VA and have offices in Seattle, WA and Washington, D.C. Our DC office positions us to work on exciting opportunities with influential organizations, such as the Gates Foundation, American Red Cross, World Bank and USAID.
