Free Drupal Trainings

For two full days on Thursday and Friday, BADCamp offers world-class training from some of the best Drupal teachers in the world. Whether you are looking to level up your front end, back end, devops, marketing automation or CRM skills, we have classes for you. 

Secure your seat today with a $20 fully-refundable deposit -- openings go quickly, and classes will fill up fast.

Thursday, October 22

Build a Module - Thursday

Skill level: Beginner

Trainers: Chris Shattuck with John Nguyen, Eric Casequin, David Hunt, David Needham, Michelle Krejci, Leslie Glynn, and Joshua Needham

The extensive BuildAModule curriculum allows you to focus on exactly what you need to know. You will only be able to work through a portion of the videos in the classroom, but we give you 8-days of access to the video library so you can start before the training and continue long after the training is over.

In the training, you can focus on one topic, or build up some basics in several topics. We will have experts that are well-versed in all of these and that can help you choose where you start.

Drupal 8 Site Building - with Views!

Skill level: Intermediate

Trainer: Ted Bowman / 6 Mile Tech

This Beginning Drupal 8 course offered by Six Mile Tech introduces you to the core concepts behind site building in Drupal 8.

Drupal 8 is the next and greatest version of Drupal. It has many improvements and will be released soon but is ready for you to start making sites with it today! This course will take you through a series of practical exercises to build a functional Drupal 8 site and prepare you to create your own dynamic websites using Drupal.

Drupal 8 Theming - Introduction

Skill level: Intermediate

Trainer: Chaz Chumley / Forum One

Master Drupal 8’s new Twig templating engine to create fun and fast websites with simple steps to help you move from concept to completion.

More Information

Drupal Multilingual for D7 and D8!

Skill level: Intermediate

Trainer: Hook 42

Drupal is a go-to CMS for both small and large websites. But, until Drupal 8, building a multilingual site in Drupal has been quite challenging. Training topics include: multilingual website concepts, Drupal 7 Multilingual overview (with a book!), Drupal 8 Hands-On Guided Training Lab.

More Information

Introduction to Headless Drupal

Skill level: Advanced

Trainers: Matt Grill and Patrick Coffey from Four Kitchens

Do you want to manage content in Drupal but use something else to deliver it to your users? This is the class for you.

We know Drupal is an amazing platform for making websites. But did you know it’s also a world-class content API that can easily be integrated with a other technologies? In this class you’ll learn how to combine Drupal with Node.js, a popular technology for building highly interactive websites, to build rich digital experiences.

More Information

Object Oriented Programming

Skill level: Beginner

Trainers: Mark Ferree, Arlina Espinoza, Brian Fisher, Jaesin Mulenex and Minnur Yunusov from Chapter Three

With the move to Drupal 8 everyone who works in the PHP layer will be exposed to more and more to object ­oriented code. Come learn the basics of working with objects in PHP and how OOP can help you to write well ­structured code.

More Information

Personalization and Dynamic Content in Drupal

Skill level: Intermediate

Trainer: Acquia

Personalization tools such as Acquia Lift have given us an unprecedented degree of control in customizing user experience. We can now server a different page to users based on their device, location and even browsing habits. How can we as developers build our sites in a way that makes it easy for all these moving parts to work together?

Drupal 8 Migration

Skill level: Intermediate

Trainer: Ryan Weal / Kafei

The upgrade path between major versions of Drupal is now more flexible and extensible than ever allowing many new ways to get content into your new Drupal 8 site. This is the result of refactoring, expanding, and porting the Migrate and Drupal-to-Drupal Data Migration modules into Drupal 8 core.

Trainer: Jesús Manuel Olivas

The hands-on training will guide on the process to build modules for Drupal 8, leveraging object-oriented techniques, modern PHP functionality as namespaces, dependency injection and new concepts introduced with Drupal 8 as the routing system, the configuration system and plugins.
