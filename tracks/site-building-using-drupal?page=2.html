We’ll introduce All the Big Things at a basic level. Attendees will come away familiar with the Drupal landscape and with recommendations for specific sessions to attend to dive deeper into key concepts and particular topics.

Examiner.com was one of the first live Drupal 7 websites and remains one of the biggest in terms of traffic and total amount of content served. Just a few years later, and we’re on the edge again.

  • Read more about How we built enterprise media publishing platform on D8

Mobile Telephony, SMS and Drupal - PowerToConnect

There are 5.6. billion regular phone users.

VoipDrupal is a suite of Drupal modules that allows a person with a regular phone to interact with your site content or shopping cart. It works with many other Drupal modules too!

Let's discuss some use cases for this actively maintained suite of modules, by looking at a project that is in-progress - PowerToConnect. What is it for? How does it work? How did it start? How did it grow? How will it continue to grow? How can you build off of it or start your own voipDrupal based project?

  • Read more about Mobile Telephony, SMS and Drupal - PowerToConnect

Drupal SEO Basics: A Drupaler's Guide to the SEO Galaxy

In case you've been living in a cave (or maybe a Vogon ship's cabin) the last few years, SEO stands for "Search Engine Optimization." Improving your website's SEO can translate into more visitors, better conversions, more sales, etc. This is why people care about it and why you should, too.

When properly configured, Drupal is a very SEO-friendly web framework. The trick is to know which Drupal SEO modules you need to install and how to optimally configure them.

R.E.A.D: Four Steps for Selecting The Right Modules

One of the most crucial and important steps in building any Drupal project is how to determine which modules to use. When you are reviewing your functionality needs you may ask yourself:

  • Where and how can I find the modules I need?
  • Will this module I found solve my functionality needs?
  • Will I need to patch this module?
  • Should I just write my own custom module?

To quickly and correctly answer these questions, there are four simple steps you should follow. It's as simple as learning how to R.E.A.D.

The Flexibility of Drupal

No other CMS offers more flexibility then Drupal. If you need to implement a piece of functionality or change a piece of data, there are a million different ways you can go about it. However, this flexibility can also be daunting and frustrating. How do you know if you are going about your changes the “right" way?

The truth of the matter is that there is no “right" way - just the “right for me” way. Any method you choose will come with it’s own sets of drawbacks and benefits.
