Lodging

Host Hotels

Berkeley hotels are filling up early for BADCamp week due to other events in the area. Please book early! We have rooms reserved in two host hotels.

1) Hotel Shattuck Plaza

Stay in the heart of Berkeley only a few blocks from UC Berkeley campus. The Hotel Shattuck Plaza building is one of the oldest hotels in Berkeley and was totally renovated just a few years ago. It's only a block from the Downtown Berkeley Bart Station, and provides easy access to the San Francisco and Oakland airports, as well as downtown SF. There are hundreds of fantastic restaurants and bars within a short walk.

We have secured a group rate, please go to http://booking.ihotelier.com/istay/istay.jsp?groupID=1487054&hotelID=17233 to book.

*Note- you can only book one room at a time using the booking website*

2) Hotel Durant 

Stay close to Berkeley campus and all the BADCamp action. The Hotel Durant was recently renovated, and is a green building with collegiate charm. It is just a few blocks away from the Downtown Berkeley Bart.

To make a reservation, its best to call the Hotel Durant at 800-238-7268 and tell them you're with Bay Area Drupal Camp.
